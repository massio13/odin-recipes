Tomato and Cheese Tart

Description

A delicious and easy savory tart made with puff pastry, fresh tomatoes, and cheese. Perfect for a quick meal or appetizer.

  • 1 puff pastry sheet
  • 4 tomatoes
  • 200 g cheese (mozzarella or goat cheese)
  • 2 tablespoons mustard
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt, pepper, and dried herbs

steps for preparing Tomato and Cheese Tart

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C (400°F).
  2. Roll out the puff pastry on a baking sheet.
  3. Spread mustard over the pastry.
  4. Slice the tomatoes and arrange them on the pastry.
  5. Add chunks of cheese on top.
  6. Season with salt, pepper, and dried herbs.
  7. Drizzle with olive oil.
  8. Bake for 20-25 minutes until the pastry is golden.
