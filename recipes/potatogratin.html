Potato Gratin

Description

A rich and creamy dish made with thinly sliced potatoes, creamy sauce, and melted cheese, baked until golden and tender.

ingredients

  • 1 kg potatoes
  • 300 ml heavy cream
  • 200 ml milk
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 100 g grated cheese (parmesan or gruyère)/li>
  • Salt, pepper, and nutmeg

steps for preparing Potato Gratin

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Peel the potatoes and slice them thinly.
  3. Rub a gratin dish with the halved garlic clove.
  4. In a bowl, mix the cream, milk, salt, pepper, and nutmeg.
  5. Layer the potato slices in the dish and pour the cream mixture over them.
  6. Top with grated cheese.
  7. Bake for 40-45 minutes until golden and the potatoes are tender.
